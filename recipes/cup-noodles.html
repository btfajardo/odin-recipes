Fried Noodles with Beef Broth


Description

This delicious noodle dish will be the star of the party. Be prepared to wow your guests with this culinary work of art. And for easy cleanup, be sure to serve your guests with the included styrofoam cup.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-packaged cup of instant noodles
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Remove the plastic packaging
  2. Open the paper lid
  3. Boil water
  4. Add hot water to the "fill" line
  5. Wait 2-3 minutes, and enjoy!
